About Hello Kitty

Hello Kitty is a fictional character produced by the Japanese company Sanrio. She is a white cat with a red bow and no visible mouth. Since her creation in 1974, Hello Kitty has become a global phenomenon and a prominent icon of cute culture.

Products

Hello Kitty products include toys, clothing, accessories, home goods and more. There are countless Hello Kitty products available, making it easy for fans to express their love for the character in various ways.

Events

Hello Kitty events are held all around the world, giving fans the opportunity to meet Hello Kitty and enjoy a variety of activities, including photo opportunities, live shows, and more. Keep an eye out for the next Hello Kitty event near you!
